# GetFlicked SEO Strategy

## Objectives
- Increase organic visibility for movie and TV show recommendation queries.
- Drive qualified traffic to the landing page and convert visitors into App Store downloads.
- Build topical authority around cinematic discovery and personalized watchlists.

## Audience & Intent Themes
- **Movie night planners** looking for curated recommendations.
- **Streaming service hoppers** searching “what to watch” or “where to stream” queries.
- **iOS power users** seeking new apps that enhance their entertainment workflow.

## Core Keyword Targets
- Primary: `movie recommendation app`, `what to watch app`, `TV show recommendation iOS`, `best movie tracker app`.
- Secondary long-tail: `personalized movie suggestions`, `find where to stream movies`, `AI movie recommendation app`, `track TV shows iPhone`, `make movie watchlist with friends`.
- Branded: `GetFlicked app`, `GetFlicked movie recommendations`, `GetFlicked watchlist`.

## Content Strategy
- Expand landing page copy with keyword-rich sections (FAQ, feature deep dives, testimonials).
- Publish supporting blog posts or guides (e.g., “How to pick tonight’s movie with GetFlicked”) targeting long-tail intent.
- Create evergreen comparison content vs. popular competitors (e.g., “GetFlicked vs. JustWatch”) to capture switchers.
- Produce seasonal/episodic content (Oscars, Halloween, holiday watchlists) to capture trend-driven searches.

## On-Page SEO Enhancements
- Optimize `<title>`, meta description, and structured headings around primary keywords.
- Add FAQ schema with common user questions (e.g., “How does GetFlicked recommend shows?”).
- Ensure image alt text references GetFlicked and feature benefits (“GetFlicked iOS watchlist interface”).
- Integrate internal links between landing sections and any blog content to reinforce keyword themes.

## Technical SEO Checklist
- Confirm Core Web Vitals pass (largest contentful paint under 2.5s, CLS < 0.1).
- Generate and submit XML sitemap & ensure robots.txt allows crawling.
- Add Open Graph & Twitter Card metadata for richer sharing.
- Implement analytics event tracking tied to download CTA clicks.

## Off-Page & Distribution
- Pitch guest posts or app review articles on entertainment and iOS blogs with backlinks to the landing page.
- Encourage users to share curated watchlists on social platforms with unique UTM links.
- Reach out to film podcasts or newsletters for featured mentions and trackable download links.
- Launch referral incentives encouraging current users to invite friends (leveraging shareable landing content).

## App Store Optimization (ASO) Alignment
- Mirror keyword set in App Store title, subtitle, and keyword fields.
- Use promo text and screenshots that reinforce landing page messaging.
- Collect and highlight user reviews; cross-link the best quotes on the website for trust signals.

## Measurement & Iteration
- Track keyword rankings weekly (primary & secondary sets).
- Monitor organic traffic, bounce rate, and download conversions in analytics dashboards.
- A/B test headline and CTA variants on the landing page for conversion lift.
- Review backlink profile monthly and prioritize high-authority opportunities.
